# Privacy Policy

RetroC IDE does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

- No telemetry, analytics, or usage data is collected.
- All files and projects remain on your local device unless you explicitly share them.
- The application does not connect to external servers except for compiler downloads you initiate.
- No third-party tracking or advertising is present.

If you have questions or concerns about privacy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
